What Is StearsReport?

StearsReport includes a weekly overview of the Nigerian financial markets and a quarterly review of evolving sectors. The weekly report analyses the performance of the Nigerian Stock Exchange (NSE), its related indices and market trends. The industry report is a critical review of trends influencing growth and innovation in a particular sector.

StearsReport also includes a glossary of definitions and functions of institutions for new entrants into the Nigerian financial markets.
