What Is StearsReport?

Each week, the StearsReport takes a look back at the events of the previous seven days on the Nigerian financial markets. Broken down into the Oil/Gas and Banking sectors, it looks at the performance of the Nigerian Stock Exchange and related indices.

It includes a glossary for new entrants to Finance and is put together by the Finance Team of the Writer’s Network.
